ABOUT ME

MY ART IS INSPIRED BY THE MYSTERIES OF THE COSMOS.

DIVINE MYTHS AND THE STRUGGLE BETWEEN CREATION AND DESTRUCTION.

I ENJOY TRANSFORMING STILL ILLUSTRATIONS

INTO LIVING, LOOPING ANIMATIONS, GIVING ART A CINEMATIC AND IMMERSIVE QUALITY.

THROUGH MY PORTFOLIO I AIM TO SHOWCASE NOT ONLY FINISHED WORKS BUT

ALSO THE PROCESS, EXPERIMENTATIONS, AND NARRATIVE DEPTH BEHIND EACH PIECE.

I AM A DIGITAL MEDIA AND INTERACTIVE ARTS ENTHUSIAST WITH A PASSION

FOR CREATING VISUAL STORIES THAT BLEND FANTASY, MYTHOLOGY, AND COSMIC ELEMENTS.

MY WORK EXPLORES THE BALANCE BETWEEN LIGHT AND DARKNESS, USING BOTH

TRADITIONAL AND DIGITAL TOOLS TO BRING CONCEPTS TO LIFE
